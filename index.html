Founded on 20+ Years of Experience

Tone Entertainment was founded by Matt Commerce - a professional musician, DJ, and entertainment industry pro, with over 20 years of real-world experience.

Across hundreds of live events, from intimate weddings to high-energy corporate galas, Matt studied the subtle details that shape an experience and mastered the art of creating moments people remember.

Fluent in the languages and needs of both performers and event planners, Matt and his team ensure expectations are aligned, communication is clear, and execution is seamless, every time.

"Amateurs practice until they get it right; professionals practice until they can’t get it wrong."

Our Philosophy

In-person connection matters more than ever. Great music pulls people into the moment, away from their screens and into real connection, creating relationships and positive associations that last a lifetime..

Southern California is as beautiful as it is talent-rich. We connect you with world-class artists and use our local expertise to help you make the most of the region’s diverse music scene, so your event sounds as memorable as the setting itself.

We’re deeply committed to sustainability and reducing the carbon footprint of the event industry. With a network of elite performers across California, we prioritize booking talent close to home, cutting unnecessary travel, lowering emissions, and reducing costs for our clients.
